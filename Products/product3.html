Flushable Paper Toilet Seat Covers

Safe, eco-friendly, and water-soluble covers with a free plastic dispenser included!

Features

  • Biodegradable, water-soluble, and eco-friendly
  • Pocket-size compact travel packs
  • Safe for all skin types, including pregnant women

Why Choose This?

Our flushable toilet seat covers are made of 100% natural virgin wood pulp. They dissolve quickly in water, preventing drain blockages, while providing a hygienic barrier between you and the toilet seat. Each pack comes with a **free plastic dispenser** that makes usage and storage convenient.

Usage Instructions

  • Tear the center while keeping one point connected
  • Pull the paper seat cover from the dispenser
  • Place the paper seat cover on the toilet; the center will drop automatically to prevent water spray
  • Sit on the paper seat cover
  • Flush after use; it dissolves in water without blocking drains
