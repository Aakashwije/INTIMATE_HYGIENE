These disposable waterproof toilet seat covers are ideal for adults (including pregnant women), elderly, and kids on the go. The waterproof layer and adhesive tapes prevent germs and wetness while ensuring comfort and hygiene.

Usage Instructions

  • Tear the adhesive sticker on the back

  • Place the paper seat cover over the toilet
  • Sit on the paper seat cover
  • Do not flush; dispose in a trash can
