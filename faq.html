Frequently Asked Questions

Product-Specific Questions

Q: What are disposable toilet seat covers?
A: Disposable toilet seat covers are single-use, hygienic covers designed to protect you from direct contact with public toilet seats. They provide an extra layer of cleanliness and peace of mind.

Q: What material are the covers made of?
A: Our covers are made from bidegradable, flushable paper that is safe for plumbing and septic systems.

Q: Are the covers flushable?
A: Yes, they are designed to break down quickly in water and can be flushed without harming your pipes or the environment.

Q: Do the covers fit all toilets?
A: Our covers are designed to fit standard toilet seat commonly found in public and private restrooms.

Hygiene & Safety

Q: Do toilet covers actually protect me from germs?
A: While toilet seats aren't the dirtiest surface in a restroom, using a cover reduces direct skin contact and provides peace of mind.It's simple and effective hygiene habit.

Q: Are your covers safe for children?
A: Absolutely. They're perfect for kids who may be more sensitive to public restrooms and can help parents feel more at ease.

Practical Use

Q: How do I use a disposable toilet seat cover?
A: Simply remove a cover, place it over the seat with the flag hanging inside the bowl, and flush after use.The falp helps the cover to stay in place.

Q: Do you offer travel packs?
A: Yes, we provide convenient packs that easily fit in your bag, car or pocket.

Environmental Concerns

Q: Are disposable toilet seat covers eco-friendly?
A: Our covers are made from biodegradable paper that breaks down quickly after flushing. They're a safer choice compared to plastic-based alternatives.

Q: What if I don't want to flush the cover?
A: You can also dispose of the cover in a trash bin. Both methods are safe.
