Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our disposable toilet seat covers, hygiene, and eco friendly solutions.

Product-Specific Questions

What are disposable toilet seat covers?
Disposable toilet seat covers are single-use, hygienic covers designed to protect you from direct contact with public toilet seats. They provide an extra layer of cleanliness and peace of mind.
What material are the covers made of?
Our covers are made from biodegradable, flushable paper that is safe for plumbing and septic systems.
Are the covers flushable?
Yes, they are designed to break down quickly in water and can be flushed without harming your pipes or the environment.
Do the covers fit all toilets?
Our covers are designed to fit standard toilet seats commonly found in public and private restrooms.

Hygiene & Safety

Do toilet covers actually protect me from germs?
While toilet seats aren't the dirtiest surface in a restroom, using a cover reduces direct skin contact and provides peace of mind. It's a simple and effective hygiene habit.
Are your covers safe for children?
Absolutely. They're perfect for kids who may be more sensitive to public restrooms and can help parents feel more at ease.

Practical Use

How do I use a disposable toilet seat cover?
Simply remove a cover, place it over the seat with the flag hanging inside the bowl, and flush after use. The flap helps the cover to stay in place.
Do you offer travel packs?
Yes, we provide convenient packs that easily fit in your bag, car, or pocket.

Environmental Concerns

Are disposable toilet seat covers eco-friendly?
Our covers are made from biodegradable paper that breaks down quickly after flushing. They're a safer choice compared to plastic-based alternatives.
What if I don't want to flush the cover?
You can also dispose of the cover in a trash bin. Both methods are safe.

Water & Labor Saving

How do Hygenc Covers save water?
The covers dissolve easily in water, reducing the need for extra toilet cleaning or heavy flushing. This helps save liters of water in public and private restrooms.
Do the covers reduce cleaning labor?
Yes! Using disposable covers prevents contamination of toilet seats, minimizing the time and effort janitors or cleaning staff spend sanitizing toilets.
Can they help facilities save on cleaning costs?
Absolutely. With less direct contact contamination and reduced spills, facilities spend less on chemicals, labor, and frequent deep cleaning.
Are there long-term environmental benefits?
By saving water, reducing chemical cleaners, and using biodegradable materials, Hygenc Covers contribute to a lower environmental footprint over time.

Intimate Hygiene Enterprises provides premium hygienic toilet seat covers, designed for comfort, safety, and eco-friendliness. Protect yourself and the environment wherever you go.
